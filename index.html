🫶 A Sonnet for My Queen, Umme Humaira 🫶

Shall I compare thy grace to brightest day?
Forgive the faults that drove my love astray,
For blind was I to dim thy sacred light,
And leave my heavy soul in endless night.

With tears unmatched by any stormy sea,
I pray to Heaven, Humi, to yield thee back to me.
No joy exists beneath the stars above,
Save for the healing of Humaira’s love.

So grant a chance to mend what hath been torn,
To greet with truer faith a golden morn.
